About Peoples Chamber

What is Peoples Chamber?

Peoples Chamber is a democratic platform that allows UK citizens to vote on real parliamentary bills. We believe that democracy shouldn't stop at general elections - every citizen should have a voice in the legislation that affects their lives.

How it works

1

Real Bills

We track actual bills from the UK Parliament, including sponsor information and legislative progress.

2

Your Vote

Register and cast your vote on any active bill - Support, Oppose, or Abstain.

3

Public Opinion

See how your views align with other citizens and track public sentiment on key issues.

Our Mission

To create a more informed and engaged democracy by giving every citizen a platform to express their views on legislation. While our votes are not legally binding, they represent the true voice of the people and can influence public discourse and political decision-making.

Data Sources

All bill information is sourced from official UK Parliament APIs and databases, ensuring accuracy and up-to-date information about legislative proceedings.
